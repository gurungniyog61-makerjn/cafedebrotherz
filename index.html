Our Story

Born from Brotherhood, Brewed with Passion

Cafe De Brotherz began as a dream shared between brothers over a late-night cup of coffee. Today, that dream pours into every cup we serve — a space where strangers become friends, and every visit feels like coming home.

We source our beans from ethical farmers across Nepal and beyond, roast in small batches, and pair each brew with homemade bites crafted fresh every morning.
